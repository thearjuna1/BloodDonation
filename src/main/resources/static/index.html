How HaemaLink Works

A secure, multi-step system designed to prevent fraud and ensure every donation reaches a verified patient.

01
🏥

Patient Submits Request

A patient or family member registers and submits a blood request with hospital details and required units.

02
📄

Documents Uploaded

Prescription and hospital admission proof are uploaded. Fraud detection limits requests per hour automatically.

03
👨‍⚕️

Doctor Verification

An admin-verified doctor reviews documents and approves the request before any donation can proceed.

04
🩸

Donor Responds

An eligible donor (90-day gap enforced) schedules a donation for the approved request.

05
🔐

Collector OTP Verification

The blood collector presents a government ID and verifies via OTP — anti-fraud protection at the point of collection.

06
✅

Donation Completed

The donor confirms completion. The donor's next eligible date is automatically updated. Audit trail is permanent.
