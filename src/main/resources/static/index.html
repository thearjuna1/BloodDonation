How HaemaLink Works

A secure, multi-step verification system ensuring every donation reaches a verified patient.

01
🏥

Patient Submits Request

Register as a patient and submit a blood request with hospital details, required units, and blood type.

02
📄

Upload Documents

Upload your prescription and hospital admission proof. Our system flags suspicious activity automatically.

03
👨‍⚕️

Doctor Verification

An admin-verified doctor reviews your documents and approves the request before any donor can respond.

04
🩸

Donor Responds

An eligible donor (90-day gap enforced) sees the approved request and schedules their donation.

05
✅

Donation Completed

The donor marks it complete. Their next eligible date updates automatically with a full audit trail.
